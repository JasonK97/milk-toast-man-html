About

Originally from Seattle, Derek Kent grew up with music as a heavy influence. From years of piano lessons to endeavors into music production, he is consistently excited to explore new ideas and sounds through the creation of music. Derek launched milk toast man, his solo music project, during his first year of medical school. His delve into music production and his training to become a physician fuel his excitement for life.
